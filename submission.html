Submissions

Home Submissions

All papers must be original and not simultaneously submitted to another journal or conference.

  • Papers must be based on unpublished original work and must be submitted to ICRTAC-2020 only. Submission implies the willingness of at least one of the authors to register and present the paper.
  • Deadlines for draft paper submission, notification of acceptance, camera-ready paper submission and early registration may be found in the Important Dates section.
  • You may contact on e-mail: chennai.icrtac@vit.ac.in if facing any problems in submission. All papers will be peer reviewed by at least three independent referees from the panel of national/international reviewers and will undergo plagiarism check.
  • Authors of accepted papers will need to sign a copyright form and must present paper at the conference. The organizers are using standard plagiarism checking tools to scan the papers and papers will be rejected at any stage if found plagiarized.

The manuscripts should have full complete 7 pages , written in English and formatted according to the Springer Proceedings Template(Word Template) and should be submitted electronically via Easy Chair.(Word Template)and should be submitted electronically via Easy Chair



ABOUT VIT

VIT Chennai is a globally engaged, competitive research enriched campus, strategically located in a city that is one of the biggest automotive and it hubs in India. Its motto is to respond by rearing students to major industrial, social, economic and environmental demands and challenges of cosmopolitan cities in India and the world in general. This eight years old campus offers undergraduate, post graduate and research programmes and draws smart students for every state in India, through a highly competitive examination.
