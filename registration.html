How to reach VIT Chennai from the Chennai Airport/ Chennai Central Railway Station/Chennai Koyambedu Bus Terminal

Location:

How to reach VIT Chennai from the Chennai Airport/ Chennai Central Railway Station/Chennai Koyambedu Bus Terminal:
VIT University Chennai Campus is located midway between Vandalur Zoo and Kelambakkam in South Chennai in the State of Tamil Nadu. It is 30 km away from Chennai International Airport, 42 km away from Chennai Central Railway Station and 39 km from Chennai Koyambedu Bus Terminal. It is easily accessible by Electric Train and Deluxe Buses from Chennai Airport, Chennai Koyamabedu Bus Terminal and Central Railway Station. The nearest Railway Station is Vandalur Zoo, which is 10 km from the campus. From Vandalur Zoo, public Van services are also available, which will stop at VIT University, Chennai campus gate.

From Chennai Airport to VIT Chennai Campus: ( 1.30 hours journey)
Option1:
Catch the electric train opposite to Airport ( Thirisoolam) and get down at Tambaram Railway station. Exit of Railway Station is Tambaram Bus Terminal. The campus can be reached by boarding MTC Bus Route No. 555, 515 from Tambaram bound for Tiruporur. Get down at VIT University Chennai bus stop.
Option 2:
Book a Ola or Uber call taxi and reach VIT Chennai campus.

From Chennai Central Railway Station to VIT Chennai Campus: ( 2 hours journey)
Option1:
Catch the electric train opposite to Central Railway Station ( Park) and get down at Tambaram Railway station. Exit of Railway Station is Tambaram Bus Terminal. The campus can be reached by boarding MTC Bus Route No. 555, 515 from Tambaram bound for Tiruporur. Get down at VIT University Chennai bus stop.
Option 2:
Book a Ola or Uber call taxi and reach VIT Chennai campus.

From Chennai Koyambedu Bus Terminal to VIT Chennai: ( 2 hours journey)
Option1:
Catch the MTC bus Route no 570 and get down at Kelamabkkam Bus stand. You can board MTC Bus Route No. 555, 515 from Tiruporur bound for Tambaram, which will stop at Kelambakkam. Get down at VIT University Chennai bus stop. Public Van services are also available from Kelambakkam, which will stop at VIT University, Chennai campus gate. Book a Ola or Uber call taxi. It is just 10 km from Kelambakkam.
Option 2:
Book a Ola or Uber call taxi and reach VIT Chennai campus.

ABOUT VIT

VIT Chennai is a globally engaged, competitive research enriched campus, strategically located in a city that is one of the biggest automotive and it hubs in India. Its motto is to respond by rearing students to major industrial, social, economic and environmental demands and challenges of cosmopolitan cities in India and the world in general. This eight years old campus offers undergraduate, post graduate and research programmes and draws smart students for every state in India, through a highly competitive examination.
